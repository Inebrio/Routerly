# Dashboard: Profile

The Profile page lets each logged-in user manage their own account settings. Access it by clicking your avatar or email address in the top-right corner of the dashboard.

---

## Changing Your Email

1. Enter your new email address in the **Email** field
2. Click **Save**

The change takes effect immediately. You will need to use the new email address on your next login.

---

## Changing Your Password

1. Enter your **Current Password**
2. Enter your **New Password** (minimum 8 characters)
3. Re-enter the new password in **Confirm New Password**
4. Click **Update Password**

Routerly hashes passwords with bcrypt. Your current session remains active after a password change.

---

## Account Information

The profile page also shows:

- Your **email address**
- Your **role** (admin, operator, viewer, or a custom role name)
- The **date your account was created**
